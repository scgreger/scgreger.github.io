My Top 10 Favorite Books

I have loved books every since I knew how to read (unless they were in Spanish; I hated reading in my second language). But as I entered high school, I fell into quite a long book slump. In the past year or so I have gotten back on the band waggon. Below are 10 of my favorite books I have read so far in the past year!
  1. The Fabric of Our Souls - K.M Moronova
  2. Entire ACOTAR Series - Sarah J Mass
  3. Lights Out - Nevessa Allen
  4. Fourth Wing - Rebecca Yarros
  5. Kindred Curse Series - Penn Cole
  6. Leave Me Behind - K.M. Moronova
  7. Haunting Adeline - H.D. Carlton
  8. Wings So Wicked - Emily Blackwood
  9. One Dark Window - Rachel Gillig
  10. I Who Have Never Known Men - Jacqueline Harpman
